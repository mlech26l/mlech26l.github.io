Now that I am freshly graduated, having a collaboration with MIT going on and got my paper accepted at the Deep RL Symposium at NIPS 2017, I though it is now the best time to start a blog where I can publish all my materials and stuff I am working on.

I really like this *publishing as a blog* way of presenting research results like [OpenAI](https://blog.openai.com/) is doing. It allows researchers to present a simple overview of a work and provides a place where researchers can publish materials that would not fit that well in an acadamic paper (e.g. animations, illustrations, etc.).

I hope that my current research will provide many results and I can fill this blog in the next few months, years.
